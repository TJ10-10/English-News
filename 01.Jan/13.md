## English Text
[本文]
The global financial landscape is shifting as central banks explore
Central Bank Digital Currencies (CBDCs). Unlike volatile cryptocurrencies,
CBDCs are digital forms of a nation's flat currency, regulated by the
government. Proponents argue that these digital tokens will reduce
transaction costs, increase financial inclusion for the unbanked, and
streamline cross-border payments. However, critics raise significant
concerns regarding data privacy and the potential impact on traditional
commercial banks. As pilot programs expand in various regions,
international cooperation remains essential to ensure that these
digital systems are compatible and secure against cyber threats in
an increasingly digital economy.

央銀行デジタル通貨（CBDC）の検討が進む中、世界の金融情勢は変化しています。
不安定な暗号資産とは異なり、CBDCは政府によって規制された国家の法定通貨の

験が拡大する中、デジタル経済においてこれらのシステムに互換性を持たせ、
サイバー脅威に対する安全性を確保するためには、国際的な協力が不可欠です。
